Who We Are

Fighting for Animals in Stratford Since 2000

Stratford Animal Rescue Society (STARS) was founded with unanimous support from the Stratford Town Council. We work hand-in-hand with Stratford Animal Control to ensure every impounded animal gets the care they deserve.

Every animal in our care receives vaccines, disease testing, spay/neuter, veterinary care, microchipping, training, enrichment — and love.
