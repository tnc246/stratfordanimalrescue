Volunteer with STARS

Every hour you give directly saves lives. STARS is entirely volunteer-run and we couldn't do what we do without our incredible community of animal champions.

Who We Work With

Making a Difference at Stratford Animal Control

STARS works in collaboration with the Stratford Police Department and Animal Control Division. This joint force has established a program vital to the operation of the Animal Control Facility.

Today, volunteers assist in the care of animals housed in the facility and provide support for various fundraising and humane education programs. Without them, we could not do all that we do!

Volunteer Orientation is held on an as-needed basis as openings become available. Due to limited capacity, please review all opportunities carefully before applying.

Before You Apply

General Guidelines

  • Volunteers must be 18 years of age or older.
  • Volunteers must commit to a minimum of 4 hours per year at our Annual Plant Sale in the spring.
  • Volunteers must possess a smartphone and download the Connectteam app — our main form of communication.
  • Complete a minimum of 12 hours of supervised training while being punctual and attending all training classes.
  • People seeking to serve court-mandated community service cannot be accommodated.
  • People seeking community service hours for other organizations cannot be accommodated.
  • People seeking to fulfill ABC Dog Training Certification requirements cannot be accommodated.
  • People with a criminal history record cannot be accommodated.
  • STARS may obtain the criminal history record of prospective volunteers.
