The Process

How Adoption Works

Adopting through STARS and Stratford Animal Control is simple. Here's what to expect.

1

Browse Pets

Explore available animals above. Dogs, cats, and more are waiting for their forever family.

2

Visit the Shelter

Come meet your potential new companion in person at Stratford Animal Control.

3

Submit an Application

Apply through the official Town of Stratford adoption page — not through Petfinder. Applications are reviewed by Animal Control.

4

Welcome Home

Once approved, take your new best friend home and change both your lives forever.
